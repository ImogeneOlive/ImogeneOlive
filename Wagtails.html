I find wagtails to be very charming birds. I love the way they flit about and bob around! Plenty of greater, more magnificent birds have been painted countless times, but I appreciate the everyday-ness of the wagtails and find their recurrent presence outside comforting enough that for this painting I wanted them to be my subject. I gave them a central and undistracted focus in this painting with a solid background and plenty of room for them to play within the canvas edges.
The board this is painted on is strong and thick with a texture that mimics canvas, and allows colours in the underlayer to peek through.
