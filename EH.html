Evening Haze is a graphite pencil drawing of a lioness on Cartridge Paper priced at £80. It has been framed with an ice-white mount surrounding the drawing, within a black wooden frame, complete with non-reflective glass.
This means the piece is finished with elegance, the mount and frame give it a refined look, but it is also protected by the glass that has anti-reflective properties. It may still reflect light at some angles, but if it is hung in a suitable position, as seen in the photographs, you can barely tell the glass is there!
As seen in the last image, included with the frame is hanging apparatus on the back, meaning it can be immediately hung in the home, no preparation is required from you as the buyer.
Including the frame, the piece is 28.7 x 23.4cm in size.
It will be shipped in a thick envelope with lots of bubble-wrap to ensure it arrives safely in the post: 1st Class Signed for within the UK, Tracked International for locations outside of the UK.
I think this piece makes an excellent small gift, or to fill a small space with an elegant piece of artwork.
