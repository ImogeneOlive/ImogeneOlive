White Stallion is a graphite drawing on bristol board sized 21 x 30 cms (8.2 x 11.8") priced at £160
Bristol board is thick, very smooth paper, and the drawing has been sealed with fixative to prevent smudging or damage to the drawing
The drawing is signed, dated, and provided with material and dimension information on the back.
The drawing will be shipped in a postal tube, 1st Class Signed for locations in the UK, Tracked International for locations outside of the UK.
